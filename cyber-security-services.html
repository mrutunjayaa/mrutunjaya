What is Cyber Security?

Cyber security is the practice of protecting systems, networks, applications, and data from cyber threats. It ensures your business runs securely and your customer data remains safe.

Why is Cyber Security Important?

Without proper security, your business risks:

  • Data breaches and customer information leaks
  • Financial losses due to ransomware or fraud
  • Loss of trust and business reputation
  • Compliance violations and legal penalties

Our Key Services

  • Vulnerability Assessment & Penetration Testing (VAPT): Simulate real attacks to find system flaws.
  • Web Application Testing (WAPT): Identify bugs like XSS, SQLi, and broken login systems.
  • API Security Testing: Secure your APIs from data leaks and weak authorization.
  • Network Penetration Testing: Check internal/external networks for misconfigurations and open ports.
  • Security Consultation: Strategic advice tailored to your business needs.
