What you were shown

  • A professional‑looking corporate login interfac1e
  • Familiar branding and security badges to increase trust
  • Calls to action that encourage immediate login

Learning points

  1. Always check the full domain and certificate — phishing pages often mimic look & feel.
  2. Hover over links and verify destinations before clicking.
  3. Be suspicious if an email urges immediate action or threatens consequences.
  4. Use multi‑factor authentication and don't reuse passwords across services.
